Love & Warmth for Children Fighting Cancer

Support The Nitalia’s Blankets Project

The Nitalia's Blankets project is a beautiful demonstration of love in action. When Nitalia started this project over a decade ago, her only mission was to bring comfort and warmth to children like her in the hospital fighting childhood cancer. To date, this project has delivered over 45,000 handmade blankets, hats, and fuzzy socks to young warriors and their families in hospitals around the country. These blankets, created with love, care, and compassion, serve as a symbol of hope and a reminder that these children are not fighting their battle alone.

The impact of this project extends far beyond the physical comfort of a warm blanket, it touches the hearts of everyone involved and brings joy to those who need it most.

Nitalia’s Blankets touch the hearts of everyone involved and serves as a reminder that we are all in this together. We are proud to continue of such a beautiful project and are consistently inspired by the dedication and kindness of those who make Nitalia's Blankets possible.
