One Person CAN Make A Difference!

Please find it in your heart to support our cause! We only have one goal, to support newly diagnosed children and their families. Your donation will help them stay together as they face this unspeakable tragedy. We do not use funds to support hospitals, build hospital rooms, build offices, or anything else like that! Your donation is used 100% for our cause of helping families directly!
