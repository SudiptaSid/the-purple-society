Healing Happens When We Stay Together!

Support The Purple Apartments Project

At The Purple Society, we understand the power of togetherness in the healing process. That's why when the Ronald Mcdonald House Charities approached us with a need to provide families with a safe and comfortable long term place to stay while their seriously ill children receive cancer treatment, we jumped at the opportunity.

We know all too well the fear and heartache of being separated from loved ones during a time of crisis, and we believe that families should never have to experience that. That's why we worked tirelessly to construct the Purple Apartments at Ronald McDonald House, providing families with a home away from home, where the entire family can stay together long term, close to their hospitalized child, and provide love and support throughout the entire journey.

This project is a testament to our commitment to bringing hope, comfort, and healing to families affected by serious illness. We're proud to be a part of this incredible initiative, and we hope to continue to build Purple Apartments across the world making a lasting difference in the lives of those who need it most."
