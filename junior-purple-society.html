You CAN Make A Difference!

Start Your Own The Junior Purple Society

This amazing student-led club empowers young people to make a real difference in the lives of children fighting cancer and their families. They're taking the reins and using their energy, passion, and drive to support children and families affected by cancer.

Through their visits to hospitals, collection and delivery of comfort items like blankets, caps, socks, and participation in awareness campaigns, they are providing comfort and support to those who need it most. These actions not only improve the lives of those affected by childhood cancer, but they also shape the future leaders of our world.

These students are learning valuable lessons in leadership, empathy, and kindness that will serve them well throughout their lives. They are seeing the impact of their actions firsthand and are inspired to continue making a difference in the world.

The Junior Purple Society is more than just a club, it is a movement. It's a place where young people come together to create positive change and make a real difference in the lives of others. It's a testament to the power of young people to create meaningful impact and a bright future for all.

So let us take a moment to appreciate the incredible work of the Junior Purple Society and the impact they are having on the world. This is a program that deserves recognition and support, and one that will continue to inspire future generations to make a difference in their communities and beyond.
